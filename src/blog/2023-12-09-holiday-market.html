Edamame Bear adores one thing above all else: edamame. Every day is a celebration of the simple joy found in munching on these vibrant green pods.

Two snakes, strangers at first glance, now coil upon a vase's surface. Their eyes meet, curiosity sparks, and slowly, they intertwine, crafting an intricate pattern of love, forever captured in the clay.

Once a dedicated high school teacher, this hamster has retired to pursue a newfound passion for clay artistry. With each delicate fold, he shapes clay into imperfect origami sculptures.

Vanilla White Blue-Eyed Cat Vase: he embodies the essence of a vanilla-white cat with captivating blue eyes. It longs for attention, yearning for affectionate strokes akin to a cat's gentle grooming. Highly intelligent and fragile in temperament, it craves emotional reassurance, seeking a place where love and care abound. Its delicate features conceal an inner vulnerability, making it a charming yet emotionally needy addition to any collection.

Fashioned after the iconic pufferfish, the Fugu Planter boasts impeccable skin care practices, indulging in the finest Korean snail slimy skincare treatments. Fugu spends hours engrossed in tarot card readings videos, nurturing an eclectic blend of interests. However, its true passion lies in a rather unique aspiration: to decode the intricacies of the bottlenose dolphin language, a dream that sets it apart within the realm of planters. Despite its outward appearance, its gentle spirit yearns for connections that transcend barriers, all while diligently nurturing its cherished green companions.
