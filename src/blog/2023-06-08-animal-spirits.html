Along with Olivia Carrol-Weisel and Rachel Lansman @rachpots , Cosmic Order will be selling on Saturday and Sunday from 10am-4pm at Mill Valley Pottery Studio. Discover the captivating world of the spirit animals. Embrace the mysteries of the non-human animal world by exploring the elegance of the Colorado River Toad Toxic Ice Cream Candle Holder and the exotic tranquility of the Tapir Incense Holder.

Let the Hungry Owl Vase breathe life into your favorite floral arrangements, while the Greedy Bear Wearing a Hat Vase adds an insatiable appetite for attention with its removable hat.

Be intrigued by the celestial themes and intricate details of the Stellar Raccoon Vase, where the raccoon's mischievous charm meets the cosmic mysteries of black holes and string theory.

The Sakura Bear Vase is adorned with melancholic sakura blossoms, paying homage to the ephemeral and intrinsic aspects of life. The vase features a single petal tear running down its cheek.

For cat lovers, the Cat Paw Vase offers a delightful homage to Toxoplasmosis Gondii.

And lastly, meet the Lion Sheep Planter, a playful twist on the animal kingdom that will leave you questioning nature's true intentions.
